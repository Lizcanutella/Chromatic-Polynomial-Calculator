Chromatic Polynomial Calculator

INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to the chromatic polynomial calculator!
  • Click and hold a node to drag it around the canvas.
  • To add a node, click the green '+' button.
  • To add an edge between two nodes, click the gray '/' button to toggle edge mode. Then click on the two nodes you wish to connect with an edge. To exit edge mode, click the gray '/' button again.
  • To remove a node or any edges, click the red '-' button to toggle remove mode. Then click on any nodes or edges you wish to remove. To exit remove mode, click the red '-' button again.
